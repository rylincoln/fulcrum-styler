Add an Existing Overlay 

Type in information about a hosted dataset to overlay it on your map.

All of the service's layers will appear here after you fill in the "URL" field above. You must choose at least one.
The URL template can contain {s} (subdomain), {z} (zoom level), {x} (x tile coordinate), and {y} (y tile coordinate). X, Y, and Z are required. An example for OpenStreetMap: http://{s}.tile.osm.org/{z}/{x}/{y}.png.
